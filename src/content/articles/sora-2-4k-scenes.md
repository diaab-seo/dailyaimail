Sora 2.0 is a significant upgrade to OpenAI's text-to-video model, now generating 4K video clips up to 60 seconds in length — a dramatic increase from the original 1080p, 20-second limit.

## New Capabilities

Beyond resolution improvements, Sora 2.0 introduces scene consistency across multiple generated clips, enabling users to build multi-shot sequences that maintain character appearance and environmental coherence.

## Creative Industry Response

Filmmakers and creative directors have noted that while Sora 2.0 is not yet a replacement for production-grade video work, it has become a legitimate tool for pre-visualization, storyboarding, and rapid concept iteration.

## Pricing and Access

Sora 2.0 is available to ChatGPT Pro subscribers and via the API. Generation costs are based on resolution and clip length, with 4K clips priced higher than the standard tier.
