Meta has released Llama 4 under a fully open-source license, making the model weights and training code freely available for commercial and research use. The release includes models ranging from 8B to 405B parameters.

## Benchmark Performance

Llama 4 405B achieves competitive performance with GPT-4o and Claude 3.5 Sonnet on standard benchmarks including MMLU, HumanEval, and GSM8K. The 70B variant offers strong performance at a size that can be run on a single high-end server.

## Licensing Changes

Unlike Llama 2's restricted commercial license, Llama 4 uses a standard Apache 2.0 license with no usage-based restrictions. This makes it significantly more attractive for commercial deployment and for organizations that cannot agree to proprietary licensing terms.

## Ecosystem Response

Within 48 hours of release, the Llama 4 weights had been downloaded over 500,000 times from Hugging Face. Community fine-tunes and quantized versions appeared within hours.
