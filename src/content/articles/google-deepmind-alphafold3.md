Google DeepMind has released AlphaFold 3 to academic and research institutions worldwide, expanding the model's capabilities beyond protein structure prediction to include DNA, RNA, and small molecule interactions.

## What's New in AlphaFold 3

The original AlphaFold 2 was limited to predicting the three-dimensional structure of proteins from amino acid sequences. AlphaFold 3 extends this to the full range of biological molecules, enabling researchers to model complex interactions between proteins and the drugs designed to target them.

## Impact on Drug Discovery

Pharmaceutical researchers at several institutions have reported that AlphaFold 3 significantly accelerates the early screening phase of drug discovery, reducing the time to identify promising molecular candidates from months to days.

## Access and Licensing

AlphaFold 3 is available for non-commercial research use through DeepMind's online server. Commercial licensing is available separately, with terms that have drawn scrutiny from the open science community.
