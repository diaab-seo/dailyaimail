## About Daily AI Mail

**Daily AI Mail** is an independent digital publication dedicated to covering the global artificial intelligence industry. Founded in 2026, the publication serves researchers, engineers, business leaders, policymakers, and curious readers who need accurate, timely, and substantive reporting on one of the most consequential technological developments of the modern era.

The publication operates under the editorial identity of a daily briefing — structured to deliver readers the most relevant AI developments each day without noise or sensationalism. Every story published on Daily AI Mail is selected, written, and reviewed against a defined set of editorial standards before publication.

---

## Our Mission

Daily AI Mail exists to make the global AI landscape legible to everyone who depends on it.

Artificial intelligence is no longer a niche field. It shapes hiring decisions, healthcare diagnostics, financial markets, national security doctrines, and the daily tools billions of people use to work and communicate. Yet coverage of AI remains fragmented — split between highly technical academic papers accessible to few, and viral headlines that sacrifice accuracy for attention.

Daily AI Mail occupies the space between those extremes. The publication's mission is to report on AI developments with the depth they deserve and the clarity readers need — whether the story concerns a new large language model, a regulatory framework in Brussels, a funding round in Silicon Valley, or an open-source release from a research lab in Beijing.

## What We Cover

Daily AI Mail publishes across five core content areas:

**AI News** — Breaking stories, company announcements, model releases, funding events, mergers, and policy decisions reported as they happen. News coverage prioritizes verified facts, named sources where available, and context that situates each story within broader industry trends.

**Research & Breakthroughs** — Summaries and analysis of significant papers from institutions including DeepMind, OpenAI, Meta AI, Google DeepMind, academic universities, and independent research groups. The publication translates technical findings into language accessible to informed general readers without sacrificing accuracy.

**AI Tools & Products** — In-depth coverage of AI-powered tools, software releases, and product updates. This includes hands-on assessments, feature comparisons, and an interactive tools directory maintained and updated on an ongoing basis.

**Statistics & Data** — A living reference section tracking verified, source-cited statistics on AI adoption, model performance benchmarks, investment figures, user numbers across major AI platforms, and workforce impact data. All statistics pages include publication dates and source attribution, and are updated when primary data changes.

**Perspectives & Analysis** — Longer-form editorial content examining AI's implications for society, the economy, science, and governance. This section draws on external contributors with domain expertise as well as the publication's own editorial team.

## Editorial Independence

Daily AI Mail is editorially independent. Coverage decisions are made solely on the basis of newsworthiness, reader relevance, and factual verifiability. The publication does not accept payment to influence editorial coverage, does not publish sponsored content labeled as editorial, and does not allow advertising relationships to affect reporting on any company or product.

Where the publication maintains commercial relationships — including display advertising, affiliate disclosure, and sponsored placement clearly labeled as such — these are fully separated from the editorial operation. Readers will always be able to distinguish paid content from editorial content on this site.

---

## Our Approach to Accuracy

Every claim published on Daily AI Mail is expected to meet a standard of factual verifiability. Writers are required to cite primary sources — official announcements, published research papers, regulatory filings, verified data providers — rather than relying on secondary aggregation. When a claim cannot be independently verified, it is either attributed explicitly to a named source or withheld from publication.

Corrections are handled transparently. When an error is identified in published content, the article is updated with a visible correction notice specifying what was changed and when. The publication does not silently alter articles after publication.

## Global Coverage, No Geographic Bias

Artificial intelligence development is a global phenomenon. Daily AI Mail covers developments from North America, Europe, East Asia, the Middle East, South Asia, and elsewhere with equal editorial weight. The publication does not treat any single geography as the default center of the AI world, and actively seeks to cover AI policy, research, and deployment in regions that receive less attention from English-language publications.
