## Cookies Policy

This Cookies Policy explains what cookies are, how Daily AI Mail uses them, and how you can manage your cookie preferences. It should be read alongside the [Privacy Policy](/privacy-policy), which provides broader context on how personal data is handled by the publication.

This policy was last updated on **15 March 2026**.

## 1. What Cookies Are

A cookie is a small text file that a website stores on your device — computer, smartphone, or tablet — when you visit it. Cookies allow websites to recognize your device across page loads and visits, remember your preferences, understand how you navigate the site, and deliver relevant functionality.

Cookies are not programs and cannot execute code or carry viruses. They are passive data files. A cookie set by dailyaimail.news can only be read by dailyaimail.news. Cookies set by third-party services embedded in the site (such as analytics platforms or video players) are governed by those services' own policies.

## 2. How We Use Cookies

Daily AI Mail uses cookies for the following purposes:

### 2.1 Strictly Necessary Cookies

These cookies are required for the website to function correctly. They do not collect personal data for tracking or analytics purposes and cannot be disabled without impairing site functionality. Examples include:

- **Session cookies** that maintain basic site operation during your visit
- **Security cookies** used by the Cloudflare infrastructure that delivers this website, which help identify and block malicious traffic

These cookies are set as part of operating a functional, secure website. No consent is required for their use.

### 2.2 Analytics Cookies

Daily AI Mail uses web analytics to understand how visitors interact with the site in aggregate. Analytics data is used to improve content selection, page performance, and site architecture. It is not used to profile individual users for commercial purposes.

Analytics cookies may collect:

- Pages visited and navigation paths
- Time spent on pages
- Geographic region (at country or city level, not precise location)
- Device type, browser, and operating system
- Referral source (what site or search engine directed you to us)

These cookies are set by analytics platforms and may be first-party (set by dailyaimail.news directly) or third-party (set by a service provider under a separate domain). Where third-party analytics services are used, IP addresses are anonymized where technically possible.

### 2.3 Functional Cookies

Functional cookies allow the website to remember choices you have made — such as your cookie consent preference or display settings — so that the site behaves in a consistent way across your visits. These cookies do not track your behavior across other websites.

### 2.4 Third-Party and Embedded Content Cookies

Daily AI Mail may embed content from third-party platforms, including video players, interactive tools, and social media posts. When this content is loaded, those third-party platforms may set their own cookies governed by their own privacy and cookie policies.

Where third-party embeds are known to set cookies, we attempt to disclose this in the relevant section of the site or through our consent mechanism. We do not control third-party cookies and recommend reviewing the privacy policies of those platforms directly.

## 4. Managing Your Cookie Preferences

You have several options for controlling how cookies are used when you visit dailyaimail.news:

### 4.1 Through Our Consent Mechanism

When you first visit the website, a cookie consent notice provides options to accept or decline non-essential cookies. Your preference is stored and applied on subsequent visits. You may change your preference at any time by accessing the cookie settings option available on the site.

### 4.2 Through Your Browser

All major browsers allow you to view, manage, and delete cookies through their settings menus. You can also configure your browser to block all cookies or to alert you when a cookie is being set. Browser-level cookie management overrides any settings configured through the site's consent mechanism.

Instructions for managing cookies in common browsers:

- **Google Chrome** — Settings → Privacy and Security → Cookies and other site data
- **Mozilla Firefox** — Settings → Privacy & Security → Cookies and Site Data
- **Apple Safari** — Settings → Privacy → Manage Website Data
- **Microsoft Edge** — Settings → Cookies and site permissions → Cookies and site data

Note that disabling all cookies may prevent some website features from functioning correctly, including remembering your cookie preference itself.

### 4.3 Opt-Out Tools for Analytics Platforms

If analytics cookies are in use by a third-party provider, that provider typically offers an opt-out mechanism. For example, Google Analytics provides a browser opt-out add-on available at tools.google.com/dlpage/gaoptout.

## 5. Do Not Track

Some browsers offer a "Do Not Track" (DNT) setting that sends a signal to websites requesting that your activity not be tracked. There is currently no agreed technical standard for how websites should respond to DNT signals. Daily AI Mail does not currently alter its cookie or analytics practices in response to DNT signals, though this position may be updated as standards develop.
